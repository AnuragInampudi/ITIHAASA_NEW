ITI HA ASA FOODS

The term "ITI HA ASA" originates from two Sanskrit words: "iti," which translates to "in this way," and "ha," which conveys the idea of “indeed” or "certainly." When these words are combined, "iti ha asa" conveys the meaning of "certainly, this is how it happened."

Welcome to Bean Board

Where Coffee Meets Comfort

At Iti ha asa Foods, we believe in more than just serving a cup of coffee; we are dedicated to crafting an experience, a haven where the rich aroma of freshly brewed coffee blends seamlessly with the warmth of a cosy atmosphere.
Introducing our passion project, the culmination of our love for coffee culture and the desire to create an unparalleled haven of comfort in the heart of Visakhapatnam - Bean Board_Visalakshi Nagar. Nestled in the picturesque city of Visakhapatnam, Bean Board_Vishalakshi Nagar was born out of a vision to offer not just a coffee shop, but a retreat where every visit is an opportunity to escape the mundane and embrace a world of soothing sips and delightful moments.
Established in December 2022, Bean Board_Visalakshi Nagar is the embodiment of our commitment to providing you with an experience that goes beyond the ordinary.
